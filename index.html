Akan-Name-Generator.

An Akan name generator is a tool that assigns traditional Akan names based on the day of the week someone was born.The Akan people are an ethnic group from Ghana and Cote dIvoire in West Africa.In Akan culture, children are traditionaly given a "day name"corresponding to the day of the week they were born.These names also indicate the child's gender.Eg if a male child was born on a Sunday he would be called Kwasi.
